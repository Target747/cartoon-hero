Sceptile

Description

Sceptile is a Grass type pokemon which is native to the Hoenn region. It is the final evolution of one of the three starters that any trainer wishing to start their pokemon journey has the option to pick. It is held in massive reverence for its agility and attack which when combined enables it to execute a multitude of deathly moves.
